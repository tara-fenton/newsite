About me

TARA FENTON

Hello there, my name is Tara Fenton. I'm an Interactive Developer based in New York City.

Growing up my favorite games were spades, putting puzzles together and finding the differences in the images in the highlights magazine.

I started in web design back when it was called multimedia. I quickly expended into development, working extensively with actionscript over several years. I've always enjoyed the community aspect of web development. I've attended many conferences and remain active in the community.

The role that I am looking for would be a role that the daily tasks change from day to day. Where I can use my previous skill set and problem solving abilities to expand into other technologies and languages. I am looking to work with a company that treats its employees as valued contributors to their product.
